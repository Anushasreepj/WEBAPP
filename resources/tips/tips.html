• Reset often in the beginning. A lot of different behaviors will emerge from the same settings in the first minute or so.

  • Increase the window size, which increases the board size. You can also zoom out and select "clip to view" in the main settings. A bigger game board usually facilitates evolution. Alternately try making the window small and increasing speed.

  • Check out the File->Export GIF option!

  • Start in Cooperative or Friendly collision mode with default settings and watch evolution take place. You should see a succession of ordered patterns as the population increases.

  • Slow down the speed and turn on Visual Sprout Mode and Outline Seeds & Young to see seeds turn into sprouts. It's better to switch back into functional sprout mode after.

  • Switch to Competitive mode and watch the territory-size go up in the stats tab. It helps if organisms aren't too simple already, or they may resist growing.

  • See Sprout Life Readme for more details.
